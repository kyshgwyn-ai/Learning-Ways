Learning Your Way

Explore the five ways to learn ICT effectively: Read, Watch, Listen, Visualize, Practice.

Five Ways to Learn

Each learning method links to an ICT subject to help you understand and practice technology skills.

Read

Study ICT theory, digital literacy, and computer fundamentals through interactive ebooks and guides.

Watch

Learn ICT concepts with tutorial videos, animations, and recorded lectures on programming and software tools.

Listen

Understand ICT topics via podcasts, audio lessons, and discussions on emerging technology trends.

Visualize

Use diagrams, flowcharts, and interactive simulations to comprehend ICT processes and problem-solving steps.

Practice

Apply ICT skills in coding exercises, software projects, and digital activities to reinforce learning.

About This Program

This ICT learning program integrates multiple ways of learning to develop digital skills, critical thinking, and future-ready competencies for students.
